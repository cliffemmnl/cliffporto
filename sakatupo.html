Satu Kakak Tujuh Ponakan

My intention for the opening credit sequence for Satu Kakak, Tujuh Ponakan is to tell a visual story that reflects the core message of "kasih itu berkorban" (love is sacrifice). Each family members have a symbolic element in their respective scene that represented their sacrifice to their family. Elements from the film—such as toys, objects from the house, or family symbols—appear throughout, seamlessly integrating with the narrative. The soundtrack, Kita Usahakan Rumah Itu, ties the visuals together, enhancing the sense of warmth, duty, and resilience that defines the film’s narrative.

Role: Director, Writer, Animator
Year: 2025
Project: Personal Project
