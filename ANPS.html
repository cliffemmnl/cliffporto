ANPS

The Association of National and Private Schools (ANPS) is a leading organization that supports and connects national and international schools across Indonesia. I led the rebranding initiative to modernize its visual identity and strengthen its digital presence. This included a complete redesign of the brand and a refreshed social media strategy—designed to reflect ANPS’s commitment to educational excellence, collaboration, and progress.
