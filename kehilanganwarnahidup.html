Kehilangan Warna Hidup

A mini project that attempts to capture the feelings of grief: denial, anger, bargaining, depression & acceptance in the frames of black and white. Yandy Laurens’ second film titled, “Jatuh Cinta Seperti di Film-film” left me an undeniable contemplation on grief, but leaves me with one important remark: "yang berat dari duka itu, hidup harus terus berjalan [...] lu ga harus move on, move with it aja"

Role: Photographer
Year: 2024
