SUWOW

SUWOW is Sampoerna University’s cohort-based competition celebrating student talent in sports and performing arts. More than just a contest, it’s a platform for unity and expression. I was trusted to lead its visual rebranding, taking inspiration from the energy of music festivals. The result is a refreshed identity—vibrant, rhythmic, and bold—designed to capture the spirit of celebration and togetherness that defines SUWOW.



Graphic Designers: Cliff E, Benning Mahamaya, Mayra S, James T, Alexandra Vannessa, Kenisha G

Role: Creative Director & Graphic Designer
Year: 2025
Project: Event Visual Direction
