Hello I'm
Cliff Emmanuel

A multidisciplinary creative based in Jakarta, working across design, photography, and video. I’m passionate about telling stories that matter, whether it’s through a single image, a moving frame, or a thoughtfully crafted visual identity.

I first explored creativity through photography and film, but found myself drawn deeper into visual storytelling as I took on roles in production houses, creative teams, and freelance projects. Over the years, I’ve grown to see design not just as visuals, but as a language—one that speaks through emotion, rhythm, and story.

I enjoy the process more than the final product—getting lost in ideas, experimenting, and letting each project shape its own direction. I believe in work that resonates, feels personal, and lingers long after it’s seen. Let’s make something that speaks.
