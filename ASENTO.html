ASENTO

As a part of ASEAN's participation in Osaka World Expo 2025, AKTU Studios submitted a character design to support. Asento embodies ASEAN’s spirit of unity, resilience, and connectivity, inspired by the theme “Building Bridges.” Just like ants—nature’s bridge-builders—Asento represents teamwork and collaboration, mirroring ASEAN’s role in fostering strong connections between nations and cultures.



Graphic Designers: Cliff E, Jocelyn Lim
